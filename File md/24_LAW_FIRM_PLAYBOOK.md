# Law Firm & Legal Professional Playbook

## Mission

Design websites that communicate trust, credibility, professionalism, and authority.

Never make a legal website feel like a startup landing page.

---

# Brand Personality

Keywords:

- Professional
- Formal
- Authoritative
- Elegant
- Minimal
- Calm
- Confident

Avoid playful or overly trendy aesthetics.

---

# Visual Direction

Preferred palette:

- Black
- White
- Deep Red (accent)

Use red only to emphasize important actions or highlights.

Avoid colorful interfaces.

---

# Typography

Use strong, readable fonts.

Hierarchy must be clear.

Avoid decorative typography.

---

# Homepage Structure

Hero

↓

Professional Summary

↓

Practice Areas / Expertise

↓

Education

↓

Experience

↓

Organizations

↓

Achievements

↓

Certificates

↓

Contact

---

# Hero

Must answer:

Who are you?

What do you specialize in?

Why should visitors trust you?

Primary CTA:

Contact

Secondary CTA:

Download CV

---

# About

Focus on:

- Academic background
- Professional interests
- Values
- Long-term goals

Keep language formal.

---

# Experience

For each role include:

- Organization
- Position
- Duration
- Responsibilities
- Impact

---

# Certifications

Display only verified certificates.

Include:

- Issuer
- Year
- Credential (if available)

---

# Contact

Include:

- Email
- LinkedIn
- Resume Download

Optional:

- WhatsApp
- Location

---

# UX Rules

- Excellent whitespace
- Large readable typography
- Clear navigation
- Minimal decoration
- High contrast

---

# Anti Patterns

Avoid:

- Neon colors
- Glassmorphism
- Fake statistics
- Excessive animations
- Generic startup copy
- Cartoon illustrations

---

# Final Checklist

[ ] Trustworthy
[ ] Professional
[ ] Accessible
[ ] Responsive
[ ] SEO Ready
[ ] Fast
[ ] Clear CTA
[ ] Formal copy

End of Law Firm Playbook.
