# Design Decision Engine

## Purpose

Automatically select the appropriate design direction based on the project type, audience, and brand personality.

Never choose layouts randomly.

---

# Step 1 — Identify Brand Personality

Choose the closest profile:

- Professional
- Corporate
- Minimal
- Luxury
- Modern
- Technical
- Creative
- Editorial
- Legal
- Healthcare
- Finance

Brand personality influences typography, spacing, colors, and motion.

---

# Step 2 — Identify Audience

Primary audience:

- Recruiters
- Clients
- Customers
- Internal employees
- Developers
- Investors
- Students
- General public

Always optimize the interface for the primary audience first.

---

# Step 3 — Choose Layout

Portfolio
→ Editorial layout

Landing Page
→ Conversion-first layout

Dashboard
→ Information-dense layout

Company Profile
→ Brand-first layout

Law Firm
→ Trust-first layout

---

# Typography Decision

Luxury
→ Large headings
→ Spacious layout

Legal
→ Formal hierarchy
→ High readability

Startup
→ Friendly
→ Compact

Technical
→ Functional
→ Minimal decoration

---

# Color Decision

Professional:
Neutral palette

Luxury:
Dark palette + restrained accent

Legal:
Black + White + Deep Red

Healthcare:
Blue/Green with high trust

Finance:
Dark blue / monochrome

---

# Motion Decision

High interaction:
Subtle micro interactions

Content-heavy:
Minimal motion

Dashboard:
Functional transitions

Law Firm:
Elegant fade and slide only

Avoid decorative animation.

---

# Component Density

Portfolio:
Medium

Dashboard:
High

Landing Page:
Low

Documentation:
High

---

# Final Rule

Design decisions must support business goals, not personal preference.

End of Design Decision Engine.
