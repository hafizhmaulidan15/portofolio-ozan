# Copywriting System

## Mission

Write copy that is clear, credible, and persuasive.

Never sound like generic AI marketing.

---

# Tone

Choose based on project type.

Portfolio
- Professional
- Confident
- Human
- Honest

Law Firm
- Formal
- Authoritative
- Precise
- Trustworthy

Startup
- Friendly
- Bold
- Simple

---

# Hero Formula

WHO

↓

WHAT YOU DO

↓

WHY IT MATTERS

↓

CTA

Example Structure

Headline

One sentence describing value.

One sentence creating trust.

Primary CTA.

Secondary CTA.

---

# Writing Rules

Use:

- Short sentences
- Active voice
- Strong verbs
- Specific outcomes

Avoid:

- Buzzwords
- Empty claims
- Keyword stuffing
- Overly dramatic language

---

# Section Framework

Every section should answer:

What?

Why?

How?

What next?

---

# CTA Rules

Good CTA:

- Contact Me
- View Projects
- Download Resume
- Schedule Consultation

Avoid vague CTAs.

---

# Credibility

Support claims with:

- Experience
- Certifications
- Measurable outcomes
- Organizations
- Awards

Never invent achievements.

---

# Portfolio About

Include:

- Background
- Current focus
- Strengths
- Interests
- Future goals

Keep it authentic.

---

# Readability

- One idea per paragraph.
- Break long text.
- Use descriptive headings.
- Keep rhythm consistent.

---

# Final Checklist

[ ] Human sounding
[ ] Professional

[ ] Consistent tone

End of Copywriting System.
